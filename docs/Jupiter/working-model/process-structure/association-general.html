Release: CX-Jupiter

Association - General alignment

Town hall​

  • Goal: Main update event for association members to address topics of relevance the whole Association.

  • Attendees:

    • Association member
    • Inivations are managed by the Associaiton Office

  • Schedule:

    • Quarterly

Association Operative Status Sync​

  • Goal: Update on general release process, changes, and communication. Additionally providing romm for feedback and overall release related questions.

  • Attendees:

    • Committee Leads
    • Expert Group Leads
    • Inivations are managed by the Associaiton Office

  • Schedule:

    • Bi-weekly
    • Thursdays 09:05 - 10:00
    • alternating with Technical Status Sync

Technical Status Sync​

  • Goal: Providing a room to address and solve technical questionsregarding individual. Synchronisation on development progress, challenges and dependencies.

  • Attendees:

    • Technical contacts of products and KITs
    • Inivations are managed by the Associaiton Office

  • Schedule:

    • Bi-weekly
    • Thursdays 09:05 - 10:00
    • alternating with Association Operative Status Sync

Sounding​

  • Goal: Update association members on recent progress, present future plans, and gather feedback from the audience. The meeting is organized by the committee individually with the support of the Associaiton Office.

  • Attendees:

    • Association member
    • Inivations are managed by the Associaiton Office

  • Schedule:

    • every 6 month by every committee
